Forensic & Clinical Services

Dr. Zully Lizarazo provides comprehensive forensic evaluations for adults involved in all stages of criminal and civil proceedings. Her expertise covers a wide range of psychological assessments and consultation services, including but not limited to:

Psychological Evaluations

  • Competency to stand trial evaluations
  • Insanity assessments
  • Psychosexual evaluations
  • PTSD and trauma-related evaluations
  • Assessment of emotional distress or psychological harm (civil cases)
  • Independent Medical (Psychological) Examinations (IME) – civil matters
  • Mitigation and pre-sentencing evaluations
  • Evaluations for developmental disorders (e.g., autism, intellectual disability)
  • Diagnostic evaluations & treatment recommendations
  • Family court evaluations
  • Certificate of merit evaluations
  • Risk assessments

Expert Witness Testimony

Dr. Lizarazo offers expert testimony in both criminal and civil court proceedings, providing clear, evidence-based opinions and explanations to assist the court in understanding psychological findings.

Case Consultation

Consultation services are available for attorneys and organizations seeking guidance on psychological issues relevant to their cases, including case strategy and review of psychological reports.

Fees & Payment

Fees will be determined by the type of service provided and the time required to complete the evaluation. For the most up-to-date information regarding fees, please contact Dr. Lizarazo directly.
